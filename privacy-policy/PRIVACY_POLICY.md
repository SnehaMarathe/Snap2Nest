# Snap2Nest Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 6, 2026

Snap2Nest is an offline photo collage app. We respect your privacy.

## Data We Collect
Snap2Nest does **not** collect, store, or share personal information on our servers.

## Photos and Camera
Snap2Nest may request access to:
- **Camera** (to let you capture photos for collages)
- **Photos/Media** (to let you select images and save collages)

All photo processing happens **on your device**. We do not upload your photos to any server.

## Permissions
Snap2Nest may use these permissions:
- **Camera**: to take photos inside the app
- **Media/Photos**: to read selected photos and save collages

## Third-Party Services
